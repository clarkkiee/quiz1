Onde-Onde

Did you know that onde-onde has its own song?

Onde onde is one of Mojokerto's culinary specialties that is a relic of the past. Onde-onde is said to have originated from China, where in the 12th century Chinese traders or nomads traveled to the archipelago and spread a lot of things, one of which was Onde-onde culinary. Now Onde-onde has undergone adjustments to local tastes, even being innovated into a delicious contemporary snack.
